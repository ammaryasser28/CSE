# NIST Cybersecurity Framework

---

## What is NIST?

**NIST** = **National Institute of Standards and Technology**  
- A U.S. government agency responsible for developing **standards and technologies**, including in cybersecurity.  
- Its goal is to help organizations protect themselves from cyber threats.

---

## Role of NIST in Cybersecurity

NIST provides a **Cybersecurity Framework** that offers structured guidelines to help organizations:

1. **Identify**  
   - Understand and recognize risks that could threaten systems and networks.

2. **Protect**  
   - Implement policies and procedures to safeguard systems and information.

3. **Detect**  
   - Develop methods to quickly identify cyber attacks or breaches.

4. **Respond**  
   - Plan how to handle and mitigate the impact of cybersecurity incidents.

5. **Recover**  
   - Restore systems and operations after an incident and improve defenses.

---

## In Simple Words

Think of **NIST** as a **roadmap for cybersecurity**:  
- It guides you to **identify risks → protect assets → detect attacks → respond effectively → and continuously improve** your security posture.
